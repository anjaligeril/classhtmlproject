Famous Destinations in Kerala

Kerala tourism offers the fantastic chance to explore the scintillating attractions and destinations that provide wonderful holidaying experiences in this spellbinding land.The beautiful backwaters of kerala are the combination of several rivers, inlets, lagoons, and canals that are connected to other coastal towns.Some of the backwaters destinations are Kumarakom, fort Kochi, Alappuzha and Thiruvallam etc.The magnetic hillstations of kerala are endowed with the mesmerizing beauty of nature comprising of astonishing view of mountains.Among all of the attractions, the mesmerizing backwaters are the idyllic location for squandering some leisure time with your families, friends and loved ones.The backwaters are dotted with the shadowy palm and coconut trees providing wonderful opportunities to closely explore the tranquil beauty of nature, marine life of several species, traditional life style of keralities and the magical world of water.The fascinating attraction in kerala are the rolling hill stations which are gifted with the amusing beauty of nature .
